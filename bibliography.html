About the Creator

Bryttany Grimes

CHI Fellow | Michigan State University

Bryttany is a graduate student at Michigan State University specializing in African history with a focus on women's labor, material culture, and colonial economic systems. Her research examines how traditional knowledge systems were transformed during periods of imperial expansion.

Research Interests:
African women's history, textile traditions, colonial economies, digital humanities, spatial analysis

Education:
PhD. in History, (in progress) Michigan State University M.A. in History, University of Toledo
B.A. in History, University of Findlay
